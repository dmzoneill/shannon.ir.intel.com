Shannon Communications Team

As part of the GPTW journey, Intel Shannon decided in late 2009 to concentrate on 3 areas to work on during 2010 Culture, Fun and Communications. The Communications team is a voluntary team comprising of people from many groups on site. The vision of the team is to facilitate the delivery of clear, valuable and timely information to everyone on the Shannon site. We also strive to link the great work our people do on a daily basis in Shannon to Intel‘s corporate objectives. We do this at the moment via our Weekly journal‘s, Bathroom Bulletin, a quarterly Shannon Update Meeting, posters and emails. We welcome any feedback and suggestions on how we can improve the work we do, so please contact a Comms rep or mail Shannon Comms Team
