Shipping and Post Process

 For any post/shipping requirements, please contact SIE Front Desk

Shipping

USA/China/Japan/Korea FedEx Within Ireland/Europe DHL
Freight: River International

The following shipping details must be forwarded to the front desk by 2.30pm at the latest in order for a shipment to leave on that day:

  • Delivery name, address and contact number

  • Number of pieces and value

  • Description of the contents

  • Country of manufacture

  • Weight of each piece

    •

    All shipments that are going outside of the E.U. need to be accompanied by a Pro-Forma invoice which will be created by reception. If what you are shipping was originally imported and you have a copy of the original shipping documents, please give them to the front desk as this will help us make sure we use the correct information for customs purposes.

    A tracking number will be given to you for each shipment.

    Post
    Post to Leixlip needs to be sent via Express Post. Daily collection from Brookvale Plaza at 2pm and from Dromore House at 3pm.

    Intel does not supply franking facilities for personal mail and does not sell stamps. If employees wish, they can leave their personal mail already franked at reception to be collected.

    Intel does not have registered post facility. Employees who wish to send something by registered post must go to an An Post branch. Instead, we can send post by courier DHL

    Goods Inward: Please let the front desk know if you are expecting any heavy or bulky shipment.
