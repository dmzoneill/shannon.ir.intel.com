Intel Shannon Fitness Centre

 

 W    Workout Attire, Hygiene & Cleaning

  • Employees must wear the appropriate clothing and shoes during each workout session. Proper closed toe athletic shoes and attire must be worn when using equipment.  Appropriate clothing may be as follows: 

    • Footwear:
             Only sneakers or rubber soled shoes that were designed for Fitness Centre use are allowed, no platform sneakers, high heel shoes, sandals, and leather or safety shoes. 

    • Clothing:
      T-shirts, sweat shirts, sports shirts are recommended. Business suit are not allowed.  Workout shorts such as cycling shorts, basketball and aerobics shorts, and jogging and Fitness Centre pants are recommended.

  • Fitness Centre users are required to bring their own towels which they will use to wipe their own sweat off the equipment that they have made use of. 

  • Those who wish to use gloves, supporter, waist trimmer…etc when they work out must bring their own. 

  • Employees can bring plain water or energy drink in closed containers into the fitness centre for drinking. No food may be consumed in the area. All employees must clean up any water that they may spill on the workout machines. 

  • Users are required to leave the shower area as they would wish to find it – Removing all personal items & cleaning the shower to an acceptable standard

  • Users have a responsibility to report any cleanliness issues to the Site Operations Team

Radio Guidelines

·       No Radio is provided – Employees who wish to use music as part of their work-out should use a personal stereo/MP3 with headphones

Group Exercise Schedule

·      All Group Exercise Schedules to be agreed with The Site Operations Team & clearly posted on the Gym Notice Board
