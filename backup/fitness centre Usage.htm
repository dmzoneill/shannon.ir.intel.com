Fitness Centre Usage

  • Fitness Centre users are held fully responsible for their own safety in every workout or fitness program.

  • Fitness Centre users must follow the equipment safety Guidelines strictly. Do not use fitness centre equipment if unaware of proper exercise technique(s) and apparatus functionality.  It is the employee’s responsibility to acquire the appropriate equipment knowledge. Refer to on-line Fitness Centre Orientation. http://ehs.intel.com/health/Documents/Fitness_Center_(00014589).swf

  • Employees assume responsibility for any injuries that occur while at the fitness centre. Injuries sustained at the Centre will be covered by your own personal insurance. All injuries occurring in the Fitness Centres should be reported immediately to Site Operations or your manager.

  • Anyone abusing Intel’s code of conduct, violating the fitness centre rules, Intel's safety and security guidelines, or Intel's harassment guideline will receive progressive discipline up to and including termination.

  • Absolutely NO alcoholic beverages, NO food, NO glass containers, NO smoking, NO illegal substances, NO spitting, and NO pets will be allowed in the centre.  Physical violence or abusive language will NOT be permitted.

  • Intel reserves the right to approach any member and halt any activity deemed unsafe.  Unsafe activities will be reported to Intel Site Operations.

  • Anyone abusing Intel’s conduct, safety, and security guidelines while participating at any fitness facilities may lose their privilege to use the facilities. Any violation of these rules may be considered a procedural violation and may include disciplinary action up to and including termination.

  • Scheduled activities or classes always have priority over other uses of the centre. If another activity is disrupting a scheduled activity or class, the scheduler of the activity or the instructor will make the decision and request that the second activity be stopped immediately. Failure to comply with the request may result in loss of the right of the individual(s) involved the use the facility.
