Purchasing Process

Procedure for purchasing goods/services

Goods are purchased via a Purchase order or by using CPC Program (Corporate Purchasing Card) for small expenses, i.e. less than €1600.

If you need to obtain a Purchase Order number
1. Contact Geraldine Reddan with the following information:

  • Supplier's name & contact details (address, phone, fax, e-mail)

  • Description of goods/services

  • Value and currency

  • Cost Centre to be charged

    • 2. Ger will check if the chosen supplier is on the system.

  • If yes, go to step 3. (Important: If the supplier is already on the system, the PO# can be obtained within 1 week.)

  • If not, the supplier will need to be set up on the system. A justification form will need to be filled out by the requestor (you) and an EFT form will need to be filled out by the supplier. Once both forms are returned to Ger, it takes on average 3 weeks to get the supplier on the system.

    • 3. Once the supplier is on the system, Ger will raise a Shopping basket on your behalf. T 2 to 3 people are required to approve the shopping basket internally. Once approved a number is assigned to the Shopping Basket. It can take up to 5 days for purchasing (in Malaysia) to generate a PO number.
    4.     Once you have been given the PO #, you can place your order with the supplier and must give him the PO # for invoicing purposes.

    Important note: Employees are not authorised to place orders with suppliers without having obtained a PO# first. If your order is urgent and you think the PO process might take too long, explain your case to Geraldine Reddan who will work to find a solution.
    -General office stationery is available from the Stationery room and we will be happy to special order items that you may need.
    -Business cards:  please send an email with your manager's approval to SIE Front Desk indicating on the mail the details you want on the card.  They take about 5 days from order to receipt.
