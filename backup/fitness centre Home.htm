Intel Shannon Fitness Centre

 

 

Fitness Centre Access 

Link to Fitness Centre Weekly Schedule

  • The Fitness centre is available to all Intel Shannon Blue Badge Employees

  • The centre is open to all Intel Blue Badge visitors – Blue Badge visitors should request badge access from reception

  • The use of the centre is not available to other members of the public or site visitors

  • The fitness centre is open to employees 24 hours a day including weekends.  The fitness centre is typically not staffed. For your safety, DO NOT WORK OUT ALONE.

  • Admittance to the centre will be by card reader.  This card reader will give automatic access to all Intel blue badge employees using your ID badge. It is the responsibility of each employee to ensure that another employee does not enter behind him/her.  Each employee must use the card reader each time they enter.

  • Badges must be readily accessible for immediate presentation upon request.  If there are any problems with the card reader, please notify site operations.

  • Employee participation in any Fitness Centre program is voluntary, and is not to be undertaken during employee’s normal working hours – lunchtime is fine. The use of the fitness centre may not interfere with an employee’s job. It’s a privilege for employees to use the Fitness Centre, not a right.

  • Due to limited space and equipment limitations, use of the Fitness Centre shall be on a first- come-first-served basis

  • For maintenance or other purpose, Fitness Centre can be closed anytime for a certain purpose with or without notice.
