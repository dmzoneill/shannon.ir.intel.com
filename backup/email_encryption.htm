Email Encryption

1. Visit pki.intel.com to get the encryption and signage certificates

   
  

2. Hover mouse pointer over "Request a Certificate" and click on "Intel Encryption"

   
  

3. Click "Yes" at the resultant screen

   
  

4. Click "Request Certificate"

   
  

5. Click "Yes

   
  

6. Click "Install this Certificate"

   
  

7. Click "Yes"

   
  

8. Click on "My Certificates" located on the left hand side of the window

   
  

9. Click on the key symbol on the very right of the table

   
  

10. Enter your Domain password, a key recovery password and confirm this password

   
  

11. Click "Download Recovered Key"

   
  

12. Click "Save" and choose your Desktop as the location

   
  

13. Click "Open"

   
  

14. Click "Next"

   
  

15. Click "Next"

   
  

16. Tick "Mark this key as exportable", enter password and then click "next"

   
  

17. Click "Next"

   
  

18. Click "Finish"

   
  

19. Click "Ok"

   
  

Now we want to Install the signature, click on "Request a certificate" then "Intel Signature"

   
  

Click "Yes"

   
  

Click "Request Certificate"

   
  

Click "Yes"

   
  

Go to "My Certificates"

   
  

Double-Click on the second green arrow in the Intel Signature section

   
  

Click "Save" and save to Desktop

   
  

Click "Open"

   
  

Click "Install Certificate"

   
  

Click "Next"

   
  

Click "Next"

   
  

Click "Finish" and then "OK"

   
   
  

In Outlook click "Tools and Trust Center", select "Email Security",
Tick the box to "Encrypt contents and attachments for outgoing messages" and click on "Settings"

   
  

If data isn't populated to the "Encryption Certificate" Field click on "choose"

   
  

Select this and click "OK". Click "OK" again. Send a test email. If you send to someone who
hasn't gone through this process it will give the option to send the mail unencrypted.
You're done!
