  Fitness Centre Equipment

  • Read the Instructions and Warnings for each piece of equipment before using them.
  • Improper use of any strength or cardiovascular equipment is strictly prohibited.
  • Buddy System or spotter is required. This is especially applicable when using strength machine or dumbbells. Do not drop free weights on the floor or bang them together.  Return weights to proper rack after use (including plates).
  • Fiddling with the strength machine cables is not allowed. It may cause injury or reduce the lifespan of the equipment.
  • Allow people to work in if you’re doing many sets. Don’t sit on the machine you’re not using or when you’re resting between sets. Let a fellow Fitness Centre employee “work in” (alternate) with you.
  • Any damage to any of the pieces of equipment must be reported to Site Operations immediately to avoid accidents and further damage to the equipment.
  • Always check/secure the locks (bolts & nuts) and weights of Fitness Centre equipment or benches prior to each use.
  • All equipment is property of Intel – DO NOT REMOVE FROM THE FITNESS CENTER 
  • Any facility maintenance issues i.e., housekeeping, lighting, temperature, locker room issues, broken mirrors, carpet issues, etc., should be reported to Site Operations.
  • Please limit use of the equipment to 30 minutes when others are waiting to use the equipment.
  • Allow others to work through when doing multiple sets on a machine.
  • Each employee is responsible for wiping off equipment after each use, please use your towel or approved and provided material.
