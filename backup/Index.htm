Intel Shannon is made up of  two campuses, Dromore House and Brookvale Plaza which is located just across the street from Dromore House.

The three main focus areas on campus working to deliver Intel’s strategies in Communications Infrastructure, Automotive and Retail.

Communications Infrastructure Division (CID) part of (CSIG) By far the largest Embedded Group in Shannon, CID is focused on our vision of “Enriching the World by Transforming Communications.”  The opportunities we are unleashing can lead to a multi bn$ business for Intel.

Key capabilities on site are Project Management, Software Design, Silicon Design and the Shannon Business Centre which is forging strategic business direction for much of Embedded's European Operations and provides technical and marketing support for European customers and the Europe / Middle East / Africa field. All major European customers are directly supported from Shannon.
